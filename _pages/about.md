Bio
------
Before moving to London, I also performed my research activities as a postdoctoral researcher under the supervision of [Prof. Philipp Berens](https://www.eye-tuebingen.de/berenslab/) at the Institute for Ophthalmic Research at the University of Tübingen, Germany. We developed deep neural networks (DNNs) for detecting prevalent eye diseases and conditions, such as diabetic retinopathy (DR), age-related macular degeneration (AMD) and epiretinal membrane (ERM), from fundus photographs or optical coherence tomography (OCT) scans. Also, we systematically evaluated their clinical relevance in terms of uncertainty and interpretability. For a complete list of my publications, please, refer to [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=ZX9sqrkAAAAJ&hl=en).
